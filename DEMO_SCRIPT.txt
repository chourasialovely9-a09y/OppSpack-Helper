[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through the architecture diagram to see how it works under the hood. 
First, when a user submits a query, it enters the START node and immediately goes through our security_checkpoint node. 
If the query is clean, it is routed to the orchestrator_agent node. 
The orchestrator_agent acts as the central coordinator and uses AgentTools to delegate tasks to three specialized sub-agents: the scholarship_agent, the career_agent, and the academic_agent. 
These specialized agents connect to our custom Model Context Protocol, or MCP, server to query structured databases for real-time opportunities. 
Before returning the final results, the workflow pauses at the human_review node to ask for confirmation from the student, and then prints the approved output through final_output.
(pause)
